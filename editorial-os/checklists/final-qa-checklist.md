# Final QA Checklist (v1 — DEPRECATED)

> **DEPRECATED as of v2.1.** This checklist predates v2 governance and does not include the 12-check pre-publish gate, AI prose fingerprint checks, Treatment 2 caps, or universal BusinessExpert mention rules.
> Use instead: `16-pre-publish-gate.md` (12-check hard gate) and `05-scoring-rubric.md` (scoring with v2.1 thresholds).
> Do not use this file as a compliance reference.

Run this checklist before publishing. Every item must pass.
## Clarity
- [ ] The main point is clear within the first two paragraphs
- [ ] Every section has a clear purpose
- [ ] No jargon is used without explanation
- [ ] Sentence structure is direct and active
## Originality
- [ ] The piece adds information gain beyond generic category content
- [ ] At least one insight, trade-off, or framing would not appear on a competitor's blog
- [ ] The editorial angle is visible and specific
## Trustworthiness
- [ ] All verified facts are sourced
- [ ] All inferred points are framed as interpretation
- [ ] All editorial judgements are marked as opinion
- [ ] All human-confirmation-needed claims have placeholders or have been confirmed
- [ ] Methodology note is present (on decision-stage pages)
- [ ] Disclosures are present where required
## Practical usefulness
- [ ] A reader in the target audience could act on this content
- [ ] Next steps or recommended actions are clear
- [ ] Fit and non-fit guidance is present
## Recommendation quality
- [ ] Recommendations are supportable and specific
- [ ] Trade-offs are visible
- [ ] No recommendation is made without reasoning
## Scanability
- [ ] Subheads are descriptive and useful
- [ ] Key information is front-loaded in sections
- [ ] Comparison tables are labelled and dated
- [ ] The page works when skimmed
## Decision value
- [ ] The piece helps the target reader make, narrow, or justify a decision
- [ ] The decision the piece supports is clearly stated
- [ ] The reader is better off having read this than not
## Authorship
- [ ] The piece sounds authored, not generated
- [ ] First person is used only where it reflects real judgement or experience
- [ ] The voice is calm, direct, expert, and honest about limits
## Rubric scores
- [ ] Clarity: ___/5
- [ ] Originality: ___/5
- [ ] Trustworthiness: ___/5
- [ ] Practical usefulness: ___/5
- [ ] Insight: ___/5
- [ ] Recommendation quality: ___/5
- [ ] Authorship: ___/5
- [ ] Scanability: ___/5
- [ ] Reader satisfaction: ___/5
- [ ] Decision value: ___/5
## Publish gate
- [ ] No score below 3
- [ ] Average score 4+
- [ ] Trustworthiness is 4 or 5 (on decision-stage pages)
- [ ] Decision value is 4 or 5 (on comparison, review, and buyer pages)
