## 1. PRIMARY LEGISLATION

### Insolvency Act 1986 (c. 45)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1986/45/contents
- **Description:** Core statute governing all personal and corporate insolvency in England and Wales, including CVAs, administration, receivership, liquidation, and IP licensing.

### Company Directors Disqualification Act 1986 (c. 46)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1986/46/contents
- **Description:** Sets out grounds and procedures for disqualifying directors (up to 15 years), including mandatory disqualification for unfit directors of insolvent companies.

### Enterprise Act 2002 (c. 40) -- Part 10 (Insolvency)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2002/40/part/10
- **Description:** Major reform introducing out-of-court administration route (Schedule B1), restricting administrative receivership, abolishing Crown preference, and reducing automatic discharge to 12 months.

### Corporate Insolvency and Governance Act 2020 (c. 12)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2020/12/contents
- **Description:** Introduced permanent moratorium for companies, restructuring plans (cross-class cram-down), and supplier contract protections; plus temporary COVID-19 measures on statutory demands and winding-up petitions.

### Small Business, Enterprise and Employment Act 2015 (c. 26)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2015/26/contents/enacted
- **Description:** Extended disqualification regime (shadow directors, compensation orders), enabled administrators to bring wrongful/fraudulent trading claims, made insolvency causes of action assignable, raised bankruptcy petition threshold to GBP 5,000.

### Rating (Coronavirus) and Directors Disqualification (Dissolved Companies) Act 2021 (c. 34)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2021/34/contents/enacted
- **Description:** Gave the Insolvency Service power to investigate and disqualify directors of dissolved companies (including retrospectively), closing the loophole used to avoid BBL scrutiny.

### Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023 (c. 56)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2023/56/contents
- **Description:** Major Companies House reform with identity verification, expanded registrar powers, information-sharing with IPs, and enhanced director disqualification regime.

### Companies Act 2006 (c. 46) -- Part 26A (Restructuring Plans)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2006/46/part/26A
- **Description:** Contains restructuring plan provisions inserted by CIGA 2020 (cross-class cram-down mechanism).

### Companies Act 2006 -- Part 31 (Dissolution and Restoration)
- **Publisher:** UK Parliament / legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2006/46/part/31
- **Description:** Governs voluntary strike-off, dissolution, administrative restoration, and court-ordered restoration.

## 2. SECONDARY LEGISLATION / STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS

### The Insolvency (England and Wales) Rules 2016 (SI 2016/1024)
- **Publisher:** legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2016/1024/contents
- **Description:** Over 900 rules setting out detailed procedure for all company and individual insolvency proceedings; replaced the 1986 Rules from 6 April 2017.

### Insolvency Rules 2016 -- Explanatory Memorandum
- **Publisher:** legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2016/1024/memorandum/contents
- **Description:** Policy background and explanation of the 2016 Rules.

### The Insolvency (England and Wales) (Amendment) Rules 2021 (SI 2021/672)
- **Publisher:** legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2021/672/contents/made
- **Description:** Amendments to the 2016 Rules.

### The Enterprise Act 2002 (Insolvency) Order 2003 (SI 2003/2096)
- **Publisher:** legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2003/2096/made
- **Description:** Consequential amendments arising from the Enterprise Act 2002 insolvency reforms.

### The Insolvency Practitioners (Amendment and Transitional Provisions) Regulations 2024
- **Publisher:** legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2024 [search for exact SI number]

- **Publisher:** legislation.gov.uk
- **URL:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2024 [search for exact SI number]
