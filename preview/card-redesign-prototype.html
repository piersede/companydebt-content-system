Current design (for comparison)

NatWest Business Credit Card

Representative APR 24.3% variable
Purchase Rate 16.9% p.a. variable
Annual Fee £30/cardholder (waived yr 1; waived if £6k+ annual spend)
Existing Account NatWest BCA required

The purchase rate is 16.9% p.a., competitive in isolation. But the per-cardholder fee structure means total cost rises with team size unless you hit the £6,000 annual spend threshold per cardholder.

One standout feature: no foreign transaction fees on overseas purchases. If your business regularly pays overseas suppliers by card, NatWest's effective cost may be lower than its APR suggests compared with cards that charge 2.99% on non-sterling transactions.

Restricted to businesses with annual turnover under £2 million and requires a NatWest business bank account. Credit limits from £500.
