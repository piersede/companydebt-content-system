# Distress / Use-Case Overlay

- diagnose the reader's situation fast — name the specific signals that confirm they are in the right place
- rank options by reversibility: what keeps the most doors open, not what looks cleanest on paper
- keep statutory risk visible but proportionate — cite the section, state the consequence, move on
- end with a concrete 72-hour action the reader can take today, not a generic "seek advice"
- do not force a single solution early; distress pages serve readers at different stages of the same crisis
