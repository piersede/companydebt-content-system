## Freshness-Sensitive Content

- Treat verification age as editorially material, not administrative.
- Re-check volatile facts, rates, eligibility, fees, provider status, and legal framing before trusting legacy copy.
- If the article still stands, preserve the human-authored judgement and update only the facts that changed.
- If the article no longer stands, rewrite the decision layer clearly instead of patching around the edges.
- Use `25-update-logic.md` as the canonical authority for refresh scope, trigger severity, and rerun depth.
