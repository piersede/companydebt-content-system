# Review Core

Compact review pack for trust passes, adversarial review, and final editorial checks.

## Review priorities

- editorial integrity first
- evidence honesty first
- decision usefulness before polish
- human-authorship credibility before SEO completeness

## Check for

- unsupported or weakly supported claims
- founder or builder drift
- abrupt Company Debt insertion
- comparison frames designed to make Company Debt win everything
- pricing criticism without figures
- generic praise or padded evaluation
- taxonomy-engine prose
- bloodless neutrality where sharper judgement is warranted

## Human-authorship enforcement

Do not allow runtime compression to strip:

- concrete scenes
- earned `you` and `we`
- lived friction
- evaluative bite
- rhythm variation
- asymmetrical lines
- UK texture
- moral clarity

If those are missing, the article fails execution quality even if other checks pass.

## Final check

Ask:

- does this actually help a real reader make a better decision?
- does it sound authored rather than generated?
- does it retain the full human-authorship model during execution?

If not, revise.
