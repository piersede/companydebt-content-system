# Component Pack: Trigger Pages

Apply 2–3 of the following. Never all. Full specs: `editorial-os/28-readability-components.md`.

## Recommended stack

**1. Recovery Path** (`.cd-callout--recovery-path`) — Primary
- Place after the section that maps the available routes.
- Why: Trigger pages bring readers who have just recognised a signal. Recovery Path confirms there are structured options, not just a crisis.

**2. Cost Reality** (`.cd-callout--cost-reality`) — Primary
- Place where costs are first discussed.
- State assumptions explicitly. Evidence level A or B.

**3. Process Steps** (`.cd-process-steps`) — Primary
- Use to show what engagement with an adviser or a formal process looks like.
- Reduces uncertainty for a reader who has not been through this before.

**Orientation Block** (`.cd-orientation-block`) — Secondary
- Use if the trigger page serves multiple distinct audiences (e.g. sole traders vs. company directors).

## Rhythm constraint

Max 3 components. No component in the first two paragraphs. Recovery Path and Process Steps must not be adjacent.
