# Component Pack: Director Risk Pages

Apply 2–3 of the following. Never all. Full specs: `editorial-os/28-readability-components.md`.

## Recommended stack

**1. Legal Exposure** (`.cd-callout--legal-exposure`) — Primary
- Place in the top third, where the primary liability first appears.
- Evidence level A only (statute or case law). QA gate: must be reviewed by a licensed IP.
- Cite the specific section (e.g. s.214 IA 1986). State the consequence. One per page.

**2. What Most Directors Miss** (`.cd-callout--directors-miss`) — Primary
- Place after the section that covers the most commonly misunderstood risk.
- Evidence level A or B. Must correct a specific, named blind spot — not a general reminder.

**3. Do / Don't Panel** (`.cd-do-dont`) — Primary
- Place after the director responsibilities section.
- 3–5 rows each side. Practical, specific, action-oriented.

**Risk Warning** (`.cd-callout--risk-warning`) — Secondary
- Use only if a specific statutory deadline or enforcement threshold applies.
- Not on the same page as a Legal Exposure box unless the risks are clearly distinct.

**Timeline Reality** (`.cd-callout--timeline-reality`) — Secondary
- Use where a time-bound trigger (e.g. 2-year lookback for preferences) is central to the director's decision.

## Rhythm constraint

Max 3 components. Legal Exposure and Risk Warning must not appear adjacent. QA gate applies to all Level A callouts.
