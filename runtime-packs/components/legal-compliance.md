# Component Pack: Legal / Compliance Pages

Apply 2–3 of the following. Never all. Full specs: `editorial-os/28-readability-components.md`.

## Recommended stack

**1. Legal Exposure** (`.cd-callout--legal-exposure`) — Primary
- Place where the primary liability or compliance obligation is introduced.
- Evidence level A only. Cite statute. QA gate: must be reviewed by a licensed IP.

**2. What Most Directors Miss** (`.cd-callout--directors-miss`) — Primary
- Place after the section that covers the most commonly misread compliance requirement.
- Evidence level A or B. Correct a specific named blind spot.

**3. Do / Don't Panel** (`.cd-do-dont`) — Primary
- Place after the director duties or compliance obligations section.
- Practical and specific. 3–5 rows each side.

**Timeline Reality** (`.cd-callout--timeline-reality`) — Secondary
- Use where a statutory deadline (e.g. filing deadline, lookback period) is material to the reader's decision.
- Evidence level A only.

## Rhythm constraint

Max 3 components. QA gate on all Legal Exposure callouts. Legal Exposure and Timeline Reality must not be adjacent.
