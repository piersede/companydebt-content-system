# Component Pack: Process Guide Pages

Apply 2–3 of the following. Never all. Full specs: `editorial-os/28-readability-components.md`.

## Recommended stack

**1. Process Steps** (`.cd-process-steps`) — Primary
- Use for the core process sequence. This is the natural home for this component.
- 3–6 steps. Place where the process begins in the page structure.
- Do not add a second Process Steps module unless the page covers two clearly distinct processes.

**2. On This Page** (`.cd-on-this-page`) — Primary
- Use on all long process guides (5+ major H2 sections).
- Place after the opening paragraphs. Desktop-sticky if the layout allows.

**3. Key Takeaway** (`.cd-key-takeaway`) — Primary
- Place after the most complex stage of the process (often the creditor or legal obligations stage).
- 1–3 sentences.

**Risk Warning** (`.cd-callout--risk-warning`) — Secondary
- Use only where a specific statutory deadline or procedural failure has serious consequences.
- Evidence level A only.

## Rhythm constraint

Max 3 components. Process Steps must not be adjacent to any other structured list. On This Page must be near the top of the content.
