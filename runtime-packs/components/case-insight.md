# Component Pack: Case Insight Pages

Apply 2–3 of the following. Never all. Full specs: `editorial-os/28-readability-components.md`.

## Recommended stack

**1. CompanyDEBT View** (`.cd-callout--companydbt-view`) — Primary
- Place after the case summary, where Company Debt's interpretation or recommendation is stated.
- Evidence level B or C. Must frame a judgement, not repeat the case facts.

**2. Key Takeaway** (`.cd-key-takeaway`) — Primary
- Place after the outcome or lessons section.
- 1–3 sentences. Distils the transferable insight for the reader's own situation.

**3. Process Steps** (`.cd-process-steps`) — Primary
- Use only if the case involved a step-by-step process that is directly transferable.
- 3–5 steps. Do not reconstruct the case narrative as steps — use only where a genuine process is being illustrated.

**Creditor Perspective** (`.cd-callout--creditor-perspective`) — Secondary
- Use where the case shows how a creditor behaved and why that matters for the reader.

## Rhythm constraint

Max 3 components. CompanyDEBT View and Key Takeaway must not be adjacent. All case-insight callouts require evidence level D (anonymised case, method and date stated) or higher.
