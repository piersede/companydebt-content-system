# Component Pack: Entity Owner Pages

Apply 2–3 of the following. Never all. Full specs: `editorial-os/28-readability-components.md`.

## Recommended stack

**1. CompanyDEBT View** (`.cd-callout--companydbt-view`) — Primary
- Place after the first major explanatory section.
- Why: Entity-owner pages need an authoritative framing of the Company Debt position — not just what the options are, but which ones we see work in practice.

**2. Recovery Path** (`.cd-callout--recovery-path`) — Primary
- Place after the options section.
- Why: Entity-owner pages map multiple rescue and closure routes. A Recovery Path box anchors the decision fork clearly.

**3. Process Steps** (`.cd-process-steps`) — Primary
- Use for "what happens next" or the service engagement process.
- 3–5 steps. Not adjacent to Recovery Path callout.

**On This Page** (`.cd-on-this-page`) — Secondary
- Use on long entity-owner pages (5+ major H2 sections).
- Place near the top, after the opening paragraphs.

**Key Takeaway** (`.cd-key-takeaway`) — Secondary
- Place after the most complex section (often the creditor recovery or legal obligations section).

## Rhythm constraint

Max 3 components. No component in the first two paragraphs. At least one prose section between any two components.
