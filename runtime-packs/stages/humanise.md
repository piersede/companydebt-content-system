# Stage Pack: Humanise

Goal:
- restore voice, authorship texture, and human markers after structural revision

Rules:
- reintroduce concrete scenes and lived operational friction where they were flattened
- make evaluative lines earn their position — remove hedged balance that dilutes the point
- restore rhythm: vary sentence length, let strong endings land
- add asymmetrical lines where the prose is too even
- recover mild UK texture where natural
- apply moral clarity where it is supported by evidence
- restore earned "you" and "we" — do not invent first-hand testing
- do not change structure, claims, or factual content — voice only
