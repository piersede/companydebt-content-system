# Stage Pack: Outline

Goal:

- build a structure that front-loads the decision, makes trade-offs visible, and supports quick director scanning

Rules:

- outline around the decision question, not just topic coverage
- follow the article-type overlay and canonical page-type structure as the default skeleton
- include where the verdict or decision tension lands — it must appear early
- make sure every major section has a job: decision, avoidance, trade-off, or escalation
- make local adjustments only after the canonical structure is applied
