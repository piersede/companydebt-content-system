# Stage Pack: Source Grounding

Goal:

- turn source material into a clear map of verified facts, inference, judgement, and unresolved gaps

Rules:

- label every load-bearing claim: verified fact, editorial judgement, inference, or flagged-for-confirmation
- identify high-risk claim categories early — personal liability, wrongful trading, HMRC timing, director duties
- flag high-risk claims explicitly so drafting can treat them correctly
- downgrade certainty when support is weak; do not flatten uncertainty
- assign volatility where claims are time-sensitive (rates, thresholds, legislation)
- note any call-out-box claims that need higher evidence levels or qualified review
- make later drafting easier by being precise now
