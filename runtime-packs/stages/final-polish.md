# Stage Pack: Final Polish

Goal:

- tighten the article after structural and trust issues are resolved

Rules:

- sharpen the opening — the first sentence of every section must pay off immediately
- improve scanability without losing argument depth
- reduce clutter and repetition
- let the strongest claim stand clearly
- keep paragraphs short, endings clean, and trade-offs visible
- keep sharp lines sharp — do not polish the article into sameness
- preserve rhythm and human texture
