# Stage Pack: Trust Pass

Goal:

- review the draft for evidence integrity, trust architecture, director-risk accuracy, entity ownership, and readability

Rules:

- treat unsupported or overclaimed material as a failure to resolve
- challenge weak confidence disguised as careful wording
- tighten the article where trust is fragile
- recommend exact fixes, not vague concerns
- verify evidence handling: every load-bearing claim is labelled or removed
- verify voice integrity: no anonymous summary tone, no AI-simulated judgement
- verify the human-authorship model survived execution: concrete scenes, earned pronoun use, evaluative bite, rhythm variation, real director consequence
- fail any article that became generic, bloodless, or over-balanced
- verify the page respects entity ownership and anti-cannibalisation rules
- verify freshness logic for volatile claims (rates, thresholds, HMRC policy)
- verify any call-out boxes meet evidence, audience, and placement rules
