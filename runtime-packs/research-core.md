# Research Core

Compact execution pack for batched research and source-grounding work.

## Priorities

- primary and current sources first
- UK specificity where relevant
- exact figures over vague summaries
- clear separation between verified facts and unresolved gaps

## Rules

- prefer structured prompts over repeated prose blocks
- centralize repeated research standards in shared helpers
- cite exact URLs and dates where applicable
- if a figure cannot be verified, flag the gap rather than estimating
